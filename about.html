ABOUT

Job-Book is a company that helps job seekers publish their job application online! Created by five BYU Information Systems students, this node/express application lets you submit your information to our company database. Don't worry, this information will not be stored in any actual database. It is just an assignment for our class!

To use this website, navigate to the "Apply" page to submit your job application. After submitting your information, it will send you to the "Submit" page where you can see the data that you inserted in our form.
